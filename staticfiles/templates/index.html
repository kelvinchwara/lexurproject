Welcome to Lexur Hotel Kisii, a Contemporary Style hotel that provides all the Comfort,convenience and Efficiency to the discerning traveler.The Hotel incorporates modern facilities and functional technology with clear focus on affordable quality suitable for a world traveler

Who we are

Welcome to Lexur Hotel, a middle class style for hospitality destination that provide teh comfort and convenience to its customers in the area of;

Lexur Hotel is located along Kisi-kilgoris road in Kisii county , administartive center of Nyanza province of Kenya. Lexur hotel is situated away from congestion of Kisii town but within a walking distance of approximately two kilometers (2km) from Kisii County head office, and currently features;
